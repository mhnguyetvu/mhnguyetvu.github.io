English-Vietnamese Translation with mBART50

1. Motivation & Challenge

While multilingual models like mBART50 offer great potential, their performance on zero-shot or few-shot translation into Vietnamese often lacks the fluency and domain specificity required for professional use. My goal with this project was to explore how fine-tuning on a curated En-Vi corpus could improve the BLEU score and semantic alignment, particularly in formal text structures.

  • Adapt the pre-trained mBART50 model for the En-Vi language pair.
  • Utilize techniques like teacher forcing and label smoothing to improve convergence.
  • Evaluate performance using BLEU and TER scores across multiple test sets.
